Movie Collection

Easily View Movies in your collection, see their descritions, and add new movies

Your Movie Collection

The Movie Collection is a fun projext that I worked on that allows the user to keep track of all their favorite movies. You can add movies via an Add New Movie form when you click on the add movie button or the nav bar tab. When you hover over a movie poster the name of that movie will come into view so you can see what the title is in case it's not on the poster. If you click on a movie poster you are brought to a details view of that specific movie where you can edit the details or navigate back to the home page.
