About Me

I'm a full-stack web development student at Emerging Digital Academy in Fargo, ND, which is a fully immersive 20-week coding bootcamp focused on FullStack JavaScript and professional development. Recently, I accepted a Front End Mobile App Developer position at Scheels, and I'm thrilled to embark on this new journey. My background includes a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from North Dakota State University and six years in craft beer brewing, where I honed problem-solving and creative skills within a tight-knit team. Seeking similar challenges, I transitioned to software development, and I'm enthusiastic about continually learning new things and pursuing personal and professional growth.
